ALL EVENTS

QLN’s stunning North County Conference Center located in Oceanside, California (coastal North County San Diego) contains event, wedding reception, and meeting space for hundreds, including a single room capacity up to 700 people.

OUR SPACE

Our 22,000 square foot facility consists of six separate meeting rooms ranging in size from 572 to 3,187 square feet and a 10,500 square foot exhibit hall. Free parking for up to 138 vehicles is also available on-site.

THE LOCATION

Our Oceanside meeting space is ideal for companies and groups in the immediate area or those looking to secure space for annual events anywhere in North County San Diego, including Oceanside, Camp Pendleton, Carlsbad, Vista, San Marcos, Encinitas, and Escondido.

EASY ACCESS

We are conveniently situated between Orange County and San Diego, and within five miles of Interstate 5 and CA Highways 78 and 76. We are walking distance to the Sprinter/NCTD. Our Oceanside event center can also easily cater the Los Angeles, Irvine, and Orange County areas as well as downtown San Diego.
